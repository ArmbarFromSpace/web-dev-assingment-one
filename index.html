PERSONAL INTERESTS & PURSUITS

  • Mixology

    My favorite cocktail must be the Negroni Sbagliato, a classic European variation that is lighter but requires precise ingredient balance.

    If you would like to make one here is a recipe.
    Ingredient Measurements
    Vermouth Rosso 1 oz
    Campari 1 oz
    Prosecco 1 oz
    Orange peel Just a strip large enough to garnish
    1. Start by adding the vermouth rosso & the Campari to a mixing glass filled with ice, stir untill chilled.
    2. Using a strainer pour into a rocks glass with fresh ice.
    3. Top with prosecco.
    4. Twist the orange peel over the glass to express its oils, then garish with it.
